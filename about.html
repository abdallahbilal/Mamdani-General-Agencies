Our Story

The company was established in 1979 as a sole proprietorship by Mr. Mohamed Bashir who is a qualified Chartered accountant and has an extensive amount of experience in major business lines like Manufacturing, Clearing & Forwarding, and Trading to mention a few the main aim was to bridge the gap between companies and individual handyman. We saw the need to bring professionalism to this profession. Earlier we would get many contracts and we would work on them successfully and keep all our clients happy but we could not tender for bigger contracts so we decided to raise our bar by giving birth to Mamdani General Agencies.



Our Team

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide quality products locally manufactured and imported from various high-quality markets around the world at the best possible price.
